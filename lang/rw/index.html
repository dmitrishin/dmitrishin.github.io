Ndi umuyobozi mukuru w'inararibonye n'injiniyeri w'ubwubatsi bw'ibikorwa remezo ufite uburambe buhambaye mu mishinga minini y'ibikorwa remezo, byuzuzwa n'ubuhanga muri siyansi y'amakuru n'ubujyanama mu isesengura. Mpuza ubumenyi mu bya gihanga n'ubuhanga mu micungire n'uburyo bwo gusesengura kugira ngo nshyire mu bikorwa imishinga migari kandi ntange serivisi z'ubujyanama mu igenamigambi.


Ibyanjye

Maze imyaka irenga 21 nkora mu bujiniyeri bw'ubwubatsi n'imicungire y'imishinga, mfite uburambe mu ngufu za nikleyeri, inganda, n'ibikorwa remezo rusange. Mu mwuga wanjye wose, nagize imyanya y'ubujiniyeri n'iy'ubuyobozi mu gushushanya, igenamigambi, imicungire y'imishinga, isesengura ry'ingaruka, ubushakashatsi n'iterambere (R&D), n'ibisubizo bya ikoranabuhanga mu bujiniyeri.

Mfite ubumenyi bwimbitse ku mishinga ya EPC (Ubujiniyeri, Gutanga amasoko, no Kubaka) haba mu rwego rw'amategeko no mu rwa tekiniki, n'ubuhanga mu gucunga ingaruka haba ku rwego rw'umushinga no ku rwego rw'ikigo. Mfite ubunararibonye bwimbitse mu nganda z'amashanyarazi zikoresha nikleyeri. Kuva mu 2016, maze igihe mpuzanya ubuhanga bwanjye mu bwubatsi na siyansi y'amakuru n'uburyo bwo kwiga kw'imashini (ML) kugira ngo nkore ubushakashatsi bushingiye ku makuru. Ubuhanga bwanjye bwa tekiniki bukubiyemo ubushobozi muri Siyansi y'Amakuru, Kwiga kw'Imashini (ML), Python, Oracle Primavera, SQL, n'andi makoranabuhanga atandukanye yo gusesengura.

Nshishikajwe n'ibi bikurikira:

  • Ubufatanye mpuzamahanga n'imishinga yambukiranya imipaka
  • Imicungire n'iterambere rya gisitarateji
  • Ubushakashatsi, isesengura, n'ubujyanama mu igenamigambi
  • Icungamishinga n'uburyo bw'icungamishinga (PM)
  • Imicungire y'ingaruka ku rwego rw'ikigo n'urw'umushinga
  • Imicungire y'amasezerano ya EPC n'Ikoranabuhanga mu by'amategeko (Legal Tech)
  • Ubwubatsi bwa Nikleyeri n'ubw'ibikorwa remezo binini
  • Siyansi y'Amakuru, Kwiga kw'Imashini (ML) na Moderize nini z'ururimi (LLMs)
