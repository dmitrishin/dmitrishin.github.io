Umphathi omkhulu onekhono kanye nonjiniyela wezakhiwo zomphakathi onolwazi olunzulu kumaphrojekthi amakhulu engqalasizinda, okuhambisana nobungcweti kwisayensi yedatha kanye nokubonisana ngokuhlaziya idatha. Ngihlanganisa ulwazi lobunjiniyela nobuchwepheshe bokuphatha kanye nezindlela zokuhlaziya ukuze ngiphumelelise amaphrojekthi amakhulu futhi nginikeze izinsizakalo zokubonisana ngamasu.


Mayelana nami

Ngisebenze kubunjiniyela bokwakha nokuphathwa kwamaphrojekthi iminyaka engaphezu kwengu-21, nginolwazi oluhlanganisa amandla enuzi, izikhungo zezimboni, kanye nengqalasizinda yomphakathi. Kuwo wonke umsebenzi wami, ngibe nezikhundla zobunjiniyela nezokuphatha ekuklameni, ekuhleleni, ekuphathweni kwamaphrojekthi, ekuhlaziyweni kobungozi, kucwaningo nentuthuko (R&D), kanye nasezixazululweni ze-IT zobunjiniyela.

Nginokuqonda okuphelele kwamaphrojekthi e-EPC (Ubunjiniyela, Ukuthola Izinto, Nokwakha) kokubili ngokomthetho nangokobuchwepheshe, nginobungcweti ekuphathweni kobungozi ezingeni lephrojekthi nelebhizinisi lonke. Nginobungcweti obujulile ezikhungweni zokuphehla amandla enuzi. Kusukela ngo-2016, bengihlanganisa ubungcweti bami bokwakha namasu esayensi yedatha kanye nokufunda komshini (machine learning) ukuze ngenze ucwaningo olususelwe kudatha. Ubungcweti bami bobuchwepheshe buhlanganisa ulwazi lwe-Data Science, Machine Learning, Python, Oracle Primavera, SQL, kanye nobuchwepheshe obuhlukahlukene bokuhlaziya.

Nginentshisekelo kulezi zindawo ezilandelayo:

  • Ukubambisana kwamazwe omhlaba kanye namaphrojekthi adlula imingcele
  • Ukuphathwa kwamasu nentuthuko
  • Ucwaningo, ukuhlaziya, nokubonisana ngamasu
  • Ukuphathwa kwamaphrojekthi & nendlela yokuphatha iphrojekthi (PM methodology)
  • Ukuphathwa kobungozi ezingeni lebhizinisi nelephrojekthi
  • Ukuphathwa kwezinkontileka ze-EPC kanye ne-Legal Tech
  • Ukwakhiwa kwezikhungo zenuzi nengqalasizinda enkulu
  • Isayensi Yedatha, Ukufunda Komshini kanye Namamodeli Olimi Amakhulu (Large language models - LLMs)
