Dɔdzikpɔlagã kple xɔtuŋutinunyala bibi si si numekɔkɔ geɖe le xɔtuɖaŋudɔ gãwo me, eye wògaɖe mɔ̃ɖaŋuŋutinunya kple numekukuŋutinunya ƒe aɖaŋuɖoɖo fia. Metsɔa mɔ̃ɖaŋuŋutinunya ƒe sidzedze kple dzikpɔkpɔ ƒe aɖaŋu kple numekukuŋutinunya ƒe mɔnuwo ƒoa ƒu tsɔ wɔa dɔ gãwo eye menaa aɖaŋuɖoɖo tso tameɖoɖowo ŋu.


Nye ŋutɔ ŋutinya

Mewɔ dɔ le xɔtuɖaŋuŋutinunya kple dɔwɔɖoɖo ƒe dzikpɔkpɔ me ƒe 21 kple edzivɔ, eye numekɔkɔ le asinye le nukliagbɔŋusẽ, dɔwɔƒewo, kple xɔtuɖaŋudɔwo me. Le nye dɔwɔwɔ katã me la, menɔ mɔ̃ɖaŋuŋutinunya kple dzikpɔkpɔ ƒe nɔƒewo le nuɖoɖo, tameɖoɖo, dɔwɔɖoɖo ƒe dzikpɔkpɔ, afɔku ƒe numekuku, numekuku kple ŋgɔyiyi (R&D), kple kɔmpiuta ƒe mɔ̃ɖaŋuwo na mɔ̃ɖaŋuŋutinunya me.

Mese egɔme bliboe tso EPC dɔwɔɖoɖowo (Ŋlɔŋlɔŋlɔŋ, Nuflefle, kple Xɔtuɖaŋu) ƒe se kple mɔ̃ɖaŋu ƒe akpawo siaa me, eye aɖaŋu le asinye le afɔkuwo dzikpɔkpɔ me le dɔwɔɖoɖo kple dɔwɔƒegã ƒe nɔƒewo siaa. Numekɔkɔ deto le asinye le nukliagbɔŋusẽ ƒe dɔwɔƒewo ŋu. Tso ƒe 2016 me la, metsɔ nye xɔtuɖaŋuŋutinunya ƒe aɖaŋu kple deta sayensi (Data Science) kple mɔ̃ ƒe nusɔsrɔ̃ (Machine Learning) ƒe mɔnuwo ƒo ƒu tsɔ wɔa numekuku si wotu ɖe deta dzi. Nye mɔ̃ɖaŋuŋutinunya ƒe aɖaŋuwo ƒe ɖewoe nye sidzedze le Data Science, Machine Learning, Python, Oracle Primavera, SQL, kple numekuku mɔ̃ɖaŋu bubuwo me.

Nusiwo doa dzidzɔ nam la woe nye:

  • Dukɔwo dome ƒe nuwɔwɔ aduadu kple dukɔwo dome dɔwɔɖoɖowo
  • Tameɖoɖo ƒe dzikpɔkpɔ kple ŋgɔyiyi
  • Numekuku, numekuku, kple aɖaŋuɖoɖo tso tameɖoɖowo ŋu
  • Dɔwɔɖoɖo ƒe dzikpɔkpɔ & PM ƒe mɔnuwo
  • Dɔwɔƒegã kple dɔwɔɖoɖo ƒe afɔku dzikpɔkpɔ
  • EPC nubabla ƒe dzikpɔkpɔ kple Se ŋuti Mɔ̃ɖaŋu (Legal Tech)
  • Nuklia kple xɔtuɖaŋudɔ gãwo ƒe tutu
  • Deta Sayensi (Data Science), Mɔ̃ ƒe Nusɔsrɔ̃ (Machine Learning) kple Gbegbɔ̃ɖeɖe ƒe Model Gãwo (Large language models)
