Ndingumphathi olawulayo onamava kunye nenjineli yoluntu enamava abanzi kwiiprojekthi ezinkulu zeziseko zophuhliso, ndongezelela ngobuchule kwisayensi yedatha kunye nokucebisa ngokuhlalutya. Ndidibanisa ulwazi lobunjineli kunye nobuchule bolawulo kunye neendlela zokuhlalutya ukuhambisa iiprojekthi ezinkulu kunye nokubonelela ngeenkonzo zokucebisa zobuchule.


Ngembali yam

Ndisebenze kubunjineli bokwakha kunye nolawulo lweeprojekthi ngaphezulu kweminyaka engama-21, ndinamava aquka amandla enyukliya, izixhobo zemizi-mveliso, kunye neziseko zophuhliso zoluntu. Kuwo wonke umsebenzi wam, ndibambe izikhundla zobunjineli nezolawulo kuyilo, ucwangciso, ulawulo lweeprojekthi, uhlalutyo lomngcipheko, uphando nophuhliso (R&D), kunye nezisombululo ze-IT zobunjineli.

Ndinokuqonda okubanzi kwiiprojekthi ze-EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) kuzo zombini iimbono zezomthetho nezobuchwepheshe, kunye nobuchule bokulawula imingcipheko kwinqanaba leprojekthi neloshishino. Ndinobuchule obunzulu kwizikhululo zamandla enyukliya. Ukusukela ngo-2016, bendidibanisa ubuchule bam bokwakha kunye nesayensi yedatha kunye neendlela zokufunda koomatshini (Machine Learning) ukuqhuba uphando oluqhutywa yidatha. Ubuchule bam bobuchwepheshe buquka ukuba nobuchule kwiSayensi yeDatha, ukuFunda kooMatshini, iPython, i-Oracle Primavera, i-SQL, kunye neetekhnoloji ezahlukeneyo zokuhlalutya.

Ndinomdla kwezi ndawo zilandelayo:

  • Intsebenziswano yamazwe ngamazwe kunye neeprojekthi eziwela imida
  • Ulawulo lobuchule nophuhliso
  • Uphando, uhlalutyo, kunye nocebiso lobuchule
  • Ulawulo lweeprojekthi kunye nendlela yolawulo lweeprojekthi (PM methodology)
  • Ulawulo lomngcipheko lweshishini nolweeprojekthi
  • Ulawulo lwezivumelwano ze-EPC kunye ne-Legal Tech (Tekhnoloji yezoMthetho)
  • Ulwakhiwo lwenyukliya nolweziseko zophuhliso ezinkulu
  • ISayensi yeDatha, ukuFunda kooMatshini kunye neeModeli zoLwimi ezinkulu (LLMs)
